WordPress Custom Development

Throughout my career I have developed many WordPress sites many of them still in use today. At my last full time position, I was in charge of developing a company wide theme that could be used by the marketing team on over six sites. For this I used the Bones WordPress Boilerplate, and a selection of customized plugins to fit the needs of the team.

In my freelance career I have hacked WordPress to do some crazy things, and most recently used it for an advanced scheduling and booking platform for a business in the healthcare sector. I am currently using WordPress to power my blog and with the JSON API plugin I am pulling the latest articles from my Blog to here in my portfolio. Feel free to view the source to see it in action.
